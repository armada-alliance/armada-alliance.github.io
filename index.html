Armada Alliance

The Armada alliance was formed in efforts to build a community of decentralized, low-cost, and energy-efficient stake pool operations on Cardano. All Stake Pools that are a part of this alliance run on either Raspberry Pis exclusively or on low power consuming ARM-based machines.
